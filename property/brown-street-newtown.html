About this property

Brown Street is one of Newtown's most attractive residential pockets tree-lined, quiet and a short stroll from King Street's vibrant cafe and arts scene. This renovated Victorian terrace delivers two generously sized bedrooms, polished floorboards throughout and a contemporary kitchen that makes the most of the natural light flowing through the rear glass doors.

The private courtyard to the rear is a simple, well-maintained space for outdoor dining and morning coffee. High original ceilings and freshly painted interiors create a bright, airy atmosphere throughout both levels, and a contemporary bathroom finishes the fit-out to a move-in-ready standard.

Newtown station is a four-minute walk, putting the Sydney CBD within eight minutes by train. King Street's cafes, bars, live music venues and independent cinema are all within easy strolling distance from the front door.

Living in Newtown

Newtown is Sydney's bohemian inner-west heartland a suburb that has consistently attracted artists, musicians, academics and creative professionals for decades. King Street forms the spine of one of Sydney's most interesting retail and hospitality strips, running from St Peters in the south all the way to Camperdown in the north. Newtown station's T1 line connections make it among the most commuter-friendly of inner-city suburbs, and rental demand has remained strong across all price brackets.
