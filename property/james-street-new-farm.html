About this property

Set in a boutique building on New Farm's celebrated James Street, this two-bedroom apartment places tenants at the centre of Brisbane's most vibrant dining, retail and cultural precinct. The ground floor of the street is home to a curated collection of the city's best restaurants, independent retailers and design studios all accessible without a car.

The apartment itself is finished to a specification befitting its address: stone benchtops, quality appliances and an open-plan living and dining zone that flows to a covered entertaining balcony. Both bedrooms are well-sized, the master with ensuite access, and the second bathroom doubles as a full-size separate bathroom.

Secure basement parking, video intercom and reverse-cycle air conditioning throughout complete a specification that will appeal to discerning professional tenants.

Living in New Farm

New Farm is Brisbane's most cosmopolitan inner suburb a leafy peninsula bounded by the Brisbane River on three sides, with a cultural life that belies its relatively modest size. The James Street precinct has evolved into one of Australia's premier lifestyle destinations, anchored by fashion flagships, acclaimed restaurants and the Brisbane Powerhouse arts centre on the riverfront. New Farm Park's fig-canopied walking paths and the Merthyr Village ferry terminal complete an inner-city lifestyle that consistently attracts and retains Brisbane's most discerning residents.
