About this property

Positioned on one of Surry Hills' most sought-after residential streets, this beautifully maintained terrace home delivers the ideal balance of heritage character and contemporary comfort. The original sandstone facade and decorative ironwork give the exterior timeless Sydney character, while inside, thoughtful renovation has created an effortlessly liveable home.

The open-plan kitchen and living zone is the heart of the home, featuring stone benchtops, quality appliances and a seamless flow to the private rear courtyard perfect for morning coffee or weekend entertaining. Bedrooms are well-proportioned with built-in wardrobes throughout, and the main bathroom has been fully updated with tiled finishes.

With a secure car space, split-system air conditioning and NBN connectivity, this is a home that works hard without demanding effort. Crown Street's famous dining strip, Bourke Street Bakery and Moore Park are all within minutes on foot.

Living in Surry Hills

Surry Hills has cemented itself as one of inner-Sydney's most desirable neighbourhoods a walkable, creative village where Victorian terraces sit alongside some of the city's best restaurants, bars and boutiques. Crown Street and Bourke Street are the twin retail and dining spines, while South Dowling Street connects residents directly to the CBD and eastern suburbs. Rental demand here is perennially strong and vacancy rates remain among the lowest in the metro area.
