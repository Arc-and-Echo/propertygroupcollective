About this property

West End's character cottages are among Brisbane's most warmly sought-after rental properties, and this three-bedroom example on Boundary Street embodies everything the suburb's loyal residents love about the area. Polished hardwood floors, a north-facing deck and a private garden create a sense of relaxed lifestyle that the inner suburb is famous for.

The kitchen has been updated with a gas cooktop and contemporary cabinetry while retaining the cottage's airy, warm proportions. Three bedrooms are arranged across the home with ceiling fans in each room, and the bathroom is clean and functional with a separate shower.

With off-street parking, a short walk to the South Bank Cultural Precinct and Boundary Street's famous weekend market and coffee scene just steps away, this is quintessential West End living.

Living in West End

West End is Brisbane's creative heartland a culturally diverse, proudly independent suburb that has maintained its character through decades of inner-city growth. Boundary Street is the suburb's social and commercial spine, lined with independent cafes, bookshops, grocery stores and one of Brisbane's best weekly markets. The adjacent South Bank Cultural Precinct, the Kurilpa Bridge cycling connection to the CBD and the regenerating Fish Lane arts district collectively make West End one of the most complete inner-city neighbourhoods in Queensland.
