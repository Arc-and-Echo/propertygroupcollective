About this property

There are few residential streetscapes in Australia more recognisable than the terrace rows of Paddington, and Cascade Street is among the finest. This two-bedroom terrace has been carefully updated to provide modern living standards while retaining the iron lacework, high ceilings and Victorian proportions that define the suburb's character.

Inside, polished floorboards and freshly painted walls set a clean, bright tone. The kitchen and bathroom have both been renovated and the courtyard garden is a private sun-trap ideal for afternoon reading. The highlight is the private roof terrace a rare addition to a terrace of this era with open sky and leafy rooftop views.

Located moments from Oxford Street's boutiques and galleries, and with easy bus connections to the CBD and Bondi Junction, this property consistently attracts strong interest.

Living in Paddington

Paddington is Sydney's quintessential inner-east village famous for its heritage terraces, independent designers, Saturday markets and Oxford Street gallery strip. Five Ways provides a restaurant and bar hub at its residential heart, while the immediate surrounds include Centennial Park, the Entertainment Quarter and a quick bus ride to Bondi Beach. Rental demand in Paddington reflects its enduring desirability as one of Sydney's most liveable postcodes.
