About this property

La Trobe Terrace is Paddington's most prestigious residential address a ridge-top boulevard of elevated Queenslanders with unobstructed city views that have made it one of Brisbane's most photographed streetscapes. This four-bedroom example sits on a generous allotment and presents the characteristic wraparound veranda, chamferboard cladding and timber floors of the era's finest domestic architecture.

The kitchen and living areas have been sensitively updated to provide contemporary functionality while preserving the character proportions and period details that define the home's value. Multiple living areas on both levels provide flexibility for families and professionals alike, and the rear outdoor entertaining zone makes the most of the elevated aspect.

With a double garage, air conditioning throughout and the ever-popular Given Terrace dining strip and La Trobe Terrace Park within walking distance, this is Paddington at its finest.

Living in Paddington

Paddington Brisbane occupies the ridgeline immediately west of the CBD, offering sweeping city views from many of its streets and a retail and dining strip (Given Terrace and La Trobe Terrace) that rivals any inner suburb in Queensland. The suburb's elevated position and intact streetscapes of heritage Queenslanders make it perennially popular with premium tenants. Rental demand is anchored by proximity to the CBD, Roma Street Parkland and the inner-west cycle corridor.
